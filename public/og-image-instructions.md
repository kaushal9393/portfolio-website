# How to create your og-image.png

1. Go to https://www.canva.com
2. Create a new design → Custom size → 1200px wide × 630px tall
3. Design it with:
   - Background: dark color matching your site (#0a0a0f or similar)
   - Large text: "Kaushal Giri"
   - Smaller text: "Full-Stack Developer"
   - Subtitle: "React · Next.js · Node.js · MongoDB"
   - Optional: add your profile photo on the right side
   - Optional: add your site URL at the bottom: girikaushal.netlify.app
4. Export as PNG
5. Rename file to: og-image.png
6. Place it at: /public/og-image.png
7. Redeploy the site

This image appears when you share your link on WhatsApp, LinkedIn,
Twitter, Discord etc.
